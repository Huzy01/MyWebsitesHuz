ECAM

An electronic centralised aircraft monitor is a system that monitors aircraft functions and relays them to the pilots.
It also produces messages detailing failures and in certain cases, lists procedures to undertake to correct the problem.

EICAS

An engine-indicating and crew-alerting system(EICAS)is an integrated system.
It is commonly used in modern Boeing aircraft to provide aircraft crew with aircraft engines and other systems instrumentation and crew notifications.
